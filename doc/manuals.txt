The LPTK manuals distributed independently from the source.
Checkout the manuals module from the cvs.

cvs -d :pserver:cvs@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/lptk checkout manuals

(or something like that)

The manuals are in OpenOffice writer format (sxw). You need an OpenOffice installed to read it.
They can be very easily converted into PDF format using OO.
